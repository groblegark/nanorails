A homebrew NES toy for the Advanced NES (a real dual-PPU hardware mod). Two picture units composite a parallax town; the engine accelerates, brakes into stations, couples coaches that change its mass, and cycles day→night. Runs on a purpose-built dual-PPU emulator written in JavaScript.
